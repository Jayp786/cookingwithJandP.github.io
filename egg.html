Egg Omelette

Ingredients:

Cooking Spray or Oil

2 Eggs

1 Tablespoon of diced peppers, onions, chives, and or tomatos

1 Tablespoon of Shredded Cheese and Milk

Pinch or Salt and Pepper

Appliances:

Microwave

Directions:

Step 1: Spray or Coat mug with oil

Step 2: Mix all ingredients together for a minute

Step 3: Microwave and Stir in 30 second intervals until cooked
