Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

Eggs

Water

Salt, Pepper, and Paprika

Chives

Ziploc Bag

Appliances:

Coffee Maker

Directions:

Step 1: Place Coffee Pot with Water and wait until water boils

Step 2: Add Eggs and boil for 15 minutes

Step 3: Remove and place in cold water

Step 4: Peel and Cut Eggs in half

Step 5: Remove yolk into Ziploc and add spices/chives

Step 6: Mix contents in Ziploc Bag, Cut the corner and pipe onto egg white halves
