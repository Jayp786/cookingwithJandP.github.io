Ramen

Ingredients:

1 pack of Instant Ramen

Water

Optional: Eggs or Vegetables.

Appliances:

Coffee Maker

Directions:

Step 1: Place Coffee Pot with Water and wait until water boils

Step 2: Add the ramen and ramen spice packet

Step 3: Serve once fully cooked

Step 4: If you want to add eggs or vegetables, cook them before hand in the water for 15 to 20 minutes
