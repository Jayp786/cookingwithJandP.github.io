Mac n Cheese

Ingredients:

1/3 Cup of Elbow Pasta

1/3 Cup of Water

1/4 Cup of Milk

1/2 Cup of Shredded Cheese

Appliances:

Microwave

Directions:

Step 1: Add pasta and water in mug and microwave for 2 minutes

Step 2: Add Milk and Cheese and microwave for 1 minute

Step 3: Mix and enjoy after resting for 1 minute.
