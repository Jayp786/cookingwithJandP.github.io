Salmon

Ingredients:

Salmon

Water

Salt, Pepper, and Lemon

Optional: Veggies

Appliances:

Coffee Maker

Directions:

Step 1: Place Coffee Pot with Water and wait until water boils

Step 2: Add Salmon/Veggies/Seasoning/Lemon and boil for 10 minutes

Step 4: Remove, Rest, and Enjoy
