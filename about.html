About Us

Jay and Parth Patel

We are college roommates who now currently reside off campus, with our own cars.
We are graduating with a computer science degree. It has been a great year for us so far.
When we were freshmen, we mostly ate dining hall food, which got boring eventually. It was also
a struggle to get access to ingredients to even make our own food, considering we had no transportation.
After COVID-19 was over, and UD was finally in person, we got a place off campus, since we had a way of trans-
portation. While living off campus, we learned to cook and make lots of new foods. If we only knew this much about
cooking our freshman year, we would have been able to make some great food, with what was available. Since we do have
an idea now, we wanted to make this website to share those recipes that could potentially help other students enjoy other
foods, rather than eating at the dining halls all day, or spending money going out.Our recipes contain ingredients easily
accessible to students without a vehicle or any other transportation to a grocery store like acme or walmart.
