Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

2 pieces of bread

Butter or Mayo

Sliced Cheese (any kind)

Appliances:

Coffee Maker

Directions:

Step 1: Spread butter or mayo onto the bread

Step 2: Add cheese in between the pieces of bread and sandwich together

Step 3: Grill each side of bread on the hot plate of Coffee Maker
