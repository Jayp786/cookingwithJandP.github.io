Oreo Mug Cake

Ingredients:

4 Oreo Cookies

1/4 Cup Milk

Appliances:

Microwave

Directions:

Step 1: Crush Oreos into a microwave safe mug

Step 2: Add Milk and Mix until combined

Step 3: Microwave from 1 minute to 1 minute 30 seconds. Rest for 2 minutes before eating.
